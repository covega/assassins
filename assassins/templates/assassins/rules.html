Rules

  • aEliminate your targets and avoid being eliminated. The last assassin remaining wins.
  • A successful assassination must go unwitnessed. Your shot cannot be witnessed by anyone, or it is invalid.
  • An assassination is "witnessed" if any person sees the stream of water and can testify on the victim's behalf.
  • You have 72 hours to eliminate your target. If you fail, you will lose.
  • An assassination may not occur while the victim is in a restroom, in an in-session class, in a place of worship, in the victim's dorm room, or in your dorm's dining hall (including eating areas).
  • A person is considered inside a room if any part of their body is inside the doorway
  • You may not tell anyone your target.
  • You may not warn your target if you are eliminated.
  • Targets may be reassigned without warning.
  • If your target times out, you will be notified and will receive a new target. Your time will not be extended.
  • Participate with honor. If you submit an assassination, it is assumed your target does not dispute it. Similarly, accept a fair assassination with honor.
  • If there is reasonable doubt about the validity of an assassination, it is considered invalid.
  • The Game Master reserves the right to arbitrarily resolve all disputes and to eliminate anyone for voilating the Assassins' Code of Honor.
  • Good luck.
